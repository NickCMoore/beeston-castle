Visiting Beeston FAQs

Are the grounds and attractions suitable for wheelchairs?


Yes, however the entrance via East gate does have a lot of cobbles due to the nature of the old building.



Will dietary needs be catered for?


We have a variety of vegetarian, vegan and gluten free products on offer in the Cafe . Please feel free to ask for the dietary sheets available.



Can I bring pets into the site?


Assistance guide dogs only are permitted on site. However we do have dog friendly weekends / days, please look out for these on our website.



Can I bring a pram or pushchair into the attraction?


You are welcome to bring prams and pushchairs into the grounds. However the ramparts may not be suitable so in order to ensure the comfort of all our visitors we ask that you leave pushchairs and buggies in the designated space at the Main Gate.



Can I take photos in the attraction?


Photography is permitted inside the Castle grounds and buildings for non-commercial private use. No photography or video is permitted inside of the library area.



If you have any other questions about your visit, please contact our Visitor Experience Team on 01234 567890 ​​​​​​and they will be happy to help or complete the enquiry form on the Contact page.
